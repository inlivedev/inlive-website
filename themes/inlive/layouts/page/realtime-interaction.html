Automate your video, audio, and data interaction with our real-time API

inLive Hub, a real-time interaction platform for multi-participants to exchange live video, audio, and data. Built on top of WebRTC SFU(Selective Forwarding Unit) technology, you can use inLive hub to develop and automate online interactions like telemedicine, online class, or customer support.

Part of inLive mission is to provide an affordable alternative for media technology, we provide a fair and transparent pricing model. We charge based on the cost structure, like bandwidth and server compute time.
