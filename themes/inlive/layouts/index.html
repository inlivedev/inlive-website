Use case

  • Webinar, meeting and conference call

    Most common use case that you already see in many apps like Zoom, Google Meet, and our own app, inLive Room.

  • Walk-in interview

    Replace a long queue of walk-in interviews, with an online walk-in video call interview. With an automatic real-time schedule estimation, it will help candidates to wait and prepare on time.

  • Call centre

    Implement an in-app calling feature that enables users to seek help conveniently without worrying about phone credits or roaming charges. The automated call distribution system equipped with a smart queue allows real-time monitoring of the call queue and efficient dispatch of multiple agents to address the calls effectively.

  • Ephemeral communication channel

    Establish a temporary communication channel that facilitates seamless communication between users without revealing their phone numbers. This solution is particularly valuable for two-sided marketplace applications, where users need to communicate effectively to perform their tasks or actions.
