Why

Issue with live streaming cost and how it's not affordable for developing countries.

The cost of live video streaming has been an issue for those living in developing countries like Indonesia, or India, where the cost of running live streaming for 1 hour with 100 viewers is the same with ~2 big macs. To show you the difference between big mac prices in different countries, these are how it looks like.

Big Mac Prices

  • United States $5.15
  • Indonesia $2.34
  • India $2.39

Taken from statistica.com

Comparison pricing between live streaming vendors.

Most live streaming services use minutes of views as their price unit. We're doing things differently. If they charge you $xx per minute view, they are charging you for the highest video resolution and bandwidth cost.

But in developing countries where most people use cheap phones with low-quality screens, they'll watch lower-resolution videos to save on internet data. That's why it's fair to charge our service using bandwidth usage. This way, you only pay for what you use.

How to get started

Here's a quick guide to getting started.

  1. 1

    Sign up for an account

    First things first, you'll need to create an account by clicking "Login" in the menu. You'll be prompted to choose between Google or GitHub to log in. Once you've completed this step, you'll be able to log in to your account.

  2. 2

    Set up your live stream API key

    Once you've logged in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. We call it "Studio". From there, you can click "Settings" in the side menu to set up your live stream API key.

  3. 3

    Develop your live stream

    To start streaming, you'll need to develop your live streaming features in your application using our SDK library. You can find detailed instructions and sample code on our developer documentation page. The documentation page also includes guides for integration, troubleshooting, and other development-related topics.

  4. 4

    Start streaming

    Once you've developed a live stream in your application, you're ready to start streaming!

We hope this guide has been helpful in getting you started to implement your live streaming with our service. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. We're always here to help!
